How Your Wedding Days Take Shape

Everything That Matters, In The Most
Beautiful Wedding Destination.

Day One (Morning)
Welcome Lunch & Arrival
Guests arrive and are greeted with custom mocktails, tropical dhol beats, and seamless room check-ins handled fully by our on-site coordinator team.
Day One (Evening)
Sunset Haldi & Mehndi
The Haldi ceremony begins under ocean palms — bright, colorful, and joyous. The celebration flows into an elegant sitar Mehndi night by the sea.
Day Two (Evening)
The High-Energy Sangeet
An incredible night of choreographed Bollywood dance shows, dynamic stage lighting setups, a gourmet Indian buffet, and non-stop music.
Day Three (Morning)
Baraat & Beachfront Vows
A colorful Baraat music procession along the sand leads to a gorgeous ocean-facing mandap for the sacred wedding vows under starlit skies.
Day Three (Evening)
Grand Reception & Party
Elegant sit-down dinner, heartfelt family toasts, sparkling cake-cutting ceremonies, and a high-octane DJ after-party by the ocean.
Day Four (Morning)
Brunch & Beachfront Shoot
A scenic couple photoshoot along the turquoise coastline followed by a relaxed, sunny farewell brunch before guests depart.
